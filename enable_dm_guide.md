# How to Enable Direct Messages with Your Bot

If you see "Sending messages to this app has been turned off," you need to enable the **Messages Tab**.

## Step 1: Go to App Settings
1. Go to [api.slack.com/apps](https://api.slack.com/apps).
2. Click on your app (**ClickUp Alerter**).

## Step 2: Enable App Home
1. In the left sidebar, click **"App Home"** (under Features).
2. Scroll down to the section **"Show Tabs"**.
3. Toggle the switch for **"Messages Tab"** to **On**.
4. Check the box: **"Allow users to send Slash commands and messages from the messages tab"**.

## Step 3: Reinstall (Crucial!)
1. Go to **"Install App"** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **"Reinstall to Workspace"**.
3. Use `Ctrl+R` (Cmd+R on Mac) in your Slack app to refresh it.

Now you should be able to type `/check-tasks` directly in the bot's DM!
