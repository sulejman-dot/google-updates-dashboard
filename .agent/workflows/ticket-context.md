---
description: Get context for a complex ticket
---

## When to Run

Use when you have a complex support ticket and need to gather all relevant context from product docs and past interactions. Trigger with `/ticket-context [issue]`.

---

## Step 1: Understand the Issue

Identify the core issue from the user's request.
Ask clarifying questions if the issue is vague.

---

## Step 2: Search Context

1. **Search Product Docs**: Check `Shared Context/Product/` for relevant features and expected behavior.
2. **Check Past Issues**: Search `Collected Context/` or ask about similar past tickets known to the user.

---

## Step 3: Synthesize Findings

Start by summarizing what the feature *should* do vs. what is reported.

> "Based on the docs, [Feature X] is designed to..."

---

## Step 4: Recommend Next Steps

Based on the synthesis:
- **Troubleshooting**: Suggest specific checks.
- **Confirmation**: If it looks like a bug, define what's needed to reproduce.
- **Response**: Draft a potential response or internal note.

---

## Step 5: Save (Optional)

If significant, save notes to a temporary scratchpad or `Collected Context/Notes/`.
