description: Prepare for escalating to engineering/product
---

## When to Run

Use when you need to escalate a bug or feature request to Engineering or Product. Trigger with `/escalation-prep`.

---

## Step 1: Gather Incident Details

Ensure you have:
1. **Impact**: How many users? Business impact?
2. **Reproduction**: Exact steps to reproduce.
3. **Evidence**: Screenshots, logs, links.

Ask the user for any missing pieces.

---

## Step 2: Check Against Product Logic

Verify with `Shared Context/Product/` to ensure this isn't intended behavior or a known limitation.

---

## Step 3: Draft Escalation Message

Draft a clear, concise message for Slack/Jira.

**Format:**
- **One-line summary**: [Title]
- **Severity**: [High/Medium/Low]
- **Context**: What happened vs. what should happen.
- **Evidence**: Links/Attachments.

---

## Step 4: Refine

Ask:
> "Does this capture the urgency correctly?"

Refine based on feedback.

---

## Step 5: Output Final Draft

Present the final text ready for copy-pasting.
