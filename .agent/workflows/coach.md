## When to Run

Use when facing a decision, challenge, or pattern you want to work through. Trigger with `/coach` or "I need to think through something".

---

## Step 1: Understand the Situation

Ask:
> "What do you need help with?"

Follow up:
- What's the situation?
- What's at stake?
- What have you already tried or considered?

---

## Step 2: Clarify the Real Issue

Often the presenting problem isn't the real issue.

> "What's really bothering you about this?"
> "If you solved this, what would be different?"

---

## Step 3: Challenge Assumptions

Push back respectfully:

> "Is that actually true?"
> "What's the evidence for that?"
> "What's another way to look at this?"
> "What would Cosmin say here?" (Check his profile in Shared Context)

---

## Step 4: Explore Options

For decisions:
- What are your real options?
- What's the cost of each?
- What does your gut say?

For challenges:
- What's in your control to change?
- Who could help?
- What's the first step?

---

## Step 5: Push for Clarity

Don't let vagueness slide:

> "What specifically will you do?"
> "By when?"
> "What's the first step?"

---

## Step 6: Close

> "Want me to follow up on this?"

If yes, note in relevant file for future reference.
