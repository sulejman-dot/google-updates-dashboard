## When to Run

Use anytime you want to capture something: an idea, observation, something you learned. Trigger with `/capture` or "let me capture something".

---

## Step 1: Listen

Let them share. Don't interrupt.

After the initial share, ask:
- "What made you want to capture this?"
- "Any context I should know?"

---

## Step 2: Extract Meaning

Go deeper than just recording:

> "What's the insight here?"
> "Is there something actionable?"
> "Who else might need to know this?"

---

## Step 3: Determine Where It Goes

| If it's about... | Save to... |
|------------------|------------|
| A client/prospect | `Collected Context/People/[Name].md` |
| A project | `Collected Context/Projects/[Name].md` |
| A process/learning | `Collected Context/Notes/` |
| Something urgent to share | Flag for Slack/escalation |

---

## Step 4: Save & Confirm

Save the capture with date stamp.

Close with:
> "Captured. Anything else you want to add?"
